A Message from Our President and CEO

Welcome to Southham Bank. The best bank in business since 1886, Working to better serve the communities where the bank operates in. As you may know, Hometown Bank and Piggy's Bank merged together under Bank holding company on August 1, 2015. We have been diligently working together since that time on a plan to build a stronger institution with the least impact to our customers. Strong bonds for us with stronger returns for our clients.
